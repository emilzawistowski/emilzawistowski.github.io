## Performance Details

**Venue:** Berghain — Hauptraum (Main Floor)  
**Event:** Klubnacht (regular Saturday night)  
**Date:** December 16, 2023  
**Time Slot:** 06:00 - 09:00 (Sunday morning, 3-hour set)  
**Crowd:** ~1,500 people (peak capacity in main room)  
**Recorded:** Yes (SoundCloud excerpt available)

## Set Overview

Sunday morning slot at Berghain's main floor, taking over from the resident and building energy toward the final peak hours. The set focused on hypnotic, driving techno with careful energy management for the marathon-dancing crowd entering their 12th+ hour.

**Musical Direction:**
- Hypnotic, minimal techno with focus on groove and texture
- 128-132 BPM range
- Analog-heavy sound selection
- Long blends and extended mixing (8-12 minute tracks)
- Building intensity across the 3-hour arc

## Technical Setup

**DJ Equipment:**
- 2× Technics SL-1210MK7 turntables
- Pioneer DJM-V10 mixer (6-channel)
- Vinyl + high-quality digital files (USB/Traktor control vinyl)

**Berghain Sound System:**
- Custom Funktion-One Resolution system
- Dante-networked spatial audio
- Multiple sub zones for bass distribution
- Acoustically optimized for the concrete industrial space

**Monitoring:**
- DJ booth monitors (mid/high Funktion-One)
- Subwoofer positioning for accurate bass reference
- Isolated booth with controlled stage volume

## Set Structure & Track Selection

### Hour 1 (06:00-07:00): Deep Grounding

**Opening energy:** Continuing from resident, maintaining hypnotic groove

**Key Tracks:**
- Dettmann — "Seduction" (Ostgut Ton)
- Recondite — "Caldera" (Dystopian)
- Shifted — "Appropriation" (Avian)
- Regis — "Speak to Me" (Downwards)

**Mixing Approach:**
- Long, layered transitions (3-4 tracks playing simultaneously)
- EQ-focused blending emphasizing bassline continuity
- Minimal effects, letting track character speak
- Building tension through subtle filter work

### Hour 2 (07:00-08:00): Rising Energy

**Mid-set development:** Gradually increasing intensity and tempo

**Key Tracks:**
- Surgeon — "Magneze" (Dynamic Tension)
- Ancient Methods — "Born of Ashes" (Ancient Methods)
- Blawan — "Ternion" (Ternesc)
- Perc — "Look What Your Love Has Done" (Perc Trax)

**Mixing Approach:**
- Tighter blends with rhythmic interplay
- Introducing tougher, more percussive elements
- Filter sweeps and resonance builds
- Maintaining groove while increasing pressure

### Hour 3 (08:00-09:00): Peak & Resolution

**Final hour:** Reaching peak intensity then gentle cooldown for next DJ

**Key Tracks:**
- Setaoc Mass — "Untitled A1" (SK_Eleven)
- Rebekah — "Distant" (Soma)
- SNTS — "Seclusion" (Sacred Court)
- Closing with atmospheric track (transitioning to next DJ)

**Mixing Approach:**
- Powerful, driving selections
- Building to climax around 08:30
- Strategic breakdown for crowd release
- Gradual energy reduction in final 15 minutes

## Crowd Response & Energy Management

**Challenges:**
- Sunday morning slot requires maintaining energy for exhausted but dedicated dancers
- Following a strong resident set
- Managing peaks without losing the room

**Observations:**
- Crowd remained fully engaged throughout 3 hours
- Peak moment around 08:15 with collective euphoria
- Successful handoff to next DJ at 09:00

## Technical Execution

**Mixing Techniques:**
- Heavy use of DJM-V10's 4-band isolator EQ
- Send effects (space echo, reverb) for transitions
- Beatmatching by ear (minimal sync dependency)
- Filter automation for tension/release
- Careful gain staging for dynamic range

**Track Selection Strategy:**
- 60% vinyl, 40% digital (high-quality WAV files)
- Mix of established classics and unreleased material
- Emphasis on analog-sounding productions
- Tracks chosen for complementary basslines and groove
