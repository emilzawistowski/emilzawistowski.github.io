## About the Podcast

*Polish Documentary Audio History* is a podcast exploring the evolution of sound design, music, and audio production in Polish documentary cinema from the 1950s to the present. Through analysis of specific films, interviews with sound designers and composers, and archival research, the podcast examines how audio shaped the documentary form in Poland.

The podcast launched in January 2022 and has released 32 episodes covering major filmmakers, movements, and techniques that defined Polish documentary sound.

**Format:** 30-45 minute episodes  
**Frequency:** Monthly  
**Language:** English (with Polish film excerpts)  
**Host & Producer:** Your Name

## Research & Methodology

The podcast is based on:
- **Archival research** at the Polish National Film Archive
- **Film analysis** using original prints when available
- **Interviews** with surviving sound professionals
- **Historical context** from film journals and production documents
- **Technical analysis** of recording equipment and techniques
