## Podcast Production

### Polish Documentary Audio History

**Ongoing podcast** (2022-present) exploring the evolution of sound design and music in Polish documentary cinema from the 1950s to present.

**Statistics:**
- **32 episodes** across 3 seasons
- **5,000+ regular listeners** in 40 countries
- **45-minute** average episode length
- **Monthly** release schedule

**Approach:**
- Archival research and film analysis
- Interviews with sound professionals
- Historical context and technical deep-dives
- High-quality production standards

**Impact:**
- Featured in Film Sound Design journal
- Used as teaching material in film schools
- Archived by Polish National Film Archive
- Nominated for Best Film Sound Podcast (2023)

**Production:**
- Complete solo production (research, recording, editing, mixing)
- Field recording at archives and film screenings
- Remote interview recording
- Music licensing and original compositions

## Sound Art & Installation

### Urban Soundscape Installation (2023)

**4-channel permanent installation** at Contemporary Art Gallery exploring sonic memories of post-industrial urban transformation.

**Concept:**
- Layered field recordings from changing neighborhoods
- Archival industrial sounds vs. contemporary ambiences
- Spatial audio creating immersive environment
- Responding to gallery architecture and visitor movement

**Technical:**
- 4-channel ambisonics system
- Motion sensors for interactive elements
- Custom Max/MSP playback engine
- Site-specific acoustic optimization

**Reception:**
- **6-month exhibition** with 10,000+ visitors
- **Positive reviews** in contemporary art press
- **Commission extended** for permanent collection
