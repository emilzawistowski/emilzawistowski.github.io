## Workflow Approach

### Pre-Production
- **Technical Rider Review** — Analyzing artist requirements and feasibility
- **System Design** — CAD modeling, speaker positioning, coverage prediction
- **Input Lists** — Channel mapping, stage plots, patch organization
- **Communication** — Artist technical contact, venue coordination

### Load-In & Setup
- **System Deployment** — Rigging, placement, power distribution
- **Patching & Routing** — Console setup, networking configuration
- **System Optimization** — Alignment, EQ, delay, measurement
- **Soundcheck** — Line check, individual channels, full mix

### During Performance
- **Mix Execution** — Dynamic mixing, scene management, effects
- **Problem Solving** — Quick troubleshooting, backup systems
- **Communication** — Monitor engineer, stage manager, artist liaison
- **Recording** — Multitrack capture for virtual soundcheck or archival

### Post-Event
- **Strike** — Organized teardown and equipment care
- **Documentation** — Show files, notes for future events
- **Client Communication** — Feedback, future bookings
